Welcome

Quick instructions on how to use StreamBet.fun.

1. Connect Wallet

Use the Connect button in the header to link your wallet. You'll need it to revive tokens and track your activity.

2. Find Tokens
  • Use Search to filter and Sort to reorder.
  • Click a token to view quick details.
  • Want a surprise? Try Random Bet to pick one at random.
3. Revive & Track
  • Open a token's modal to enter an amount and get a live quote.
  • Confirm to send the transaction and watch status update.
  • See active revivals and progress in the Revivals section below.
